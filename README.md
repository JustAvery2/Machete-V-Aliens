Avery Holsinger, Jathin Banala, Nick Greco, Mason Niemas
Game Name: Machete V. Aliens

Instructions
Use AD to move.
Use SPACE to jump.
LEFT CLICK to attack.
Use WS when touching vines to climb.
The gate will open for progression when all enemies are killed.
Mash E to escape flower grasp.
“Flowering” enemies will merge when colliding with each other.

Cheat Codes
Press ENTER to open gate in current room.
Press UP ARROW to progress to next room.

Theme
The project theme of evolution was implemented both narratively and mechanically. Narratively, the story behind the game includes an alien invasion whose arbiters are attempting to bring humanity to their next stage of evolution (ie. joining their hivemind). Mechanically, the game shows evolution through the smaller enemies joining (evolving) into a larger enemy.
